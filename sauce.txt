【 🏺 Sauce & Paste Guide 】
 
* Spicy Sauce: Traditional chili oil. More fragrant 
  than spicy. Highly recommended!
* Sweet Bean Sauce: Sweet bean sauce is a mild sauce 
  with a sweet and savory flavor.
* Furu sauce: Fermented Bean Curd Sauce is made 
  from fermented tofu. It has a strong salty and savory 
  flavor.
