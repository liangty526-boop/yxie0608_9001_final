      (=====)       *  .  . *     (_______)   
   Chicken Strips   Pork Floss   Chinese Ham Sausage

* Chicken Strips: Chicken strips are pieces of seasoned 
  chicken, usually pan-fried or deep-fried before being 
  added. They make the jianbing more savory, meaty, and 
  filling.
* Pork Floss: Pork floss is dried shredded pork that is 
  seasoned and cooked until fluffy.
* Chinese Ham Sausage: Chinese ham sausage is a common 
  processed sausage in China, often made with meat and 
  starch.
