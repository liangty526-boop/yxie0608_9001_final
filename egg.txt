Eggs are the golden base of any authentic Jianbing!
* 1 Egg (Classic): The traditional street standard.
  The egg is cracked onto the jianbing batter and 
  spread together with it while cooking. It adds a 
  richer flavor and makes the crepe softer and more 
  fragrant.
* 2 Eggs (Deluxe): Double Egg Jianbing uses two eggs 
  instead of one. It has a stronger egg flavor, a 
  richer texture, and feels more filling.
