This adds the essential "crunch" to your Jianbing!
* Bo Cui (Cracker): Bo Cui is a thin and crispy fried 
  cracker often added inside jianbing. It gives the 
  jianbing a crunchy texture, and it is lighter and 
  crispier than You Tiao.
* You Tiao (Dough Stick): You Tiao is a traditional 
  Chinese deep-fried dough stick. It is crispy on the 
  outside and soft inside, and it makes the jianbing 
  more filling and chewy
